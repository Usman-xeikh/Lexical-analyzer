> A full‑featured lexical analyzer (tokenizer) for Python / C++ source code.  
> Includes a **C++ command‑line version** and an **interactive web demo** with a dark IDE theme.

---

## 🌟 Features

- ✅ **Tokenization** of keywords, identifiers, numbers (decimal, hex, octal, binary), strings (single/double/triple‑quoted), comments (single‑line and multi‑line), operators, separators, and delimiters.
- ✅ **Line‑by‑line** tracking and **error reporting** for invalid tokens.
- ✅ **Export tokens** to CSV for further analysis.
- ✅ **Interactive web demo** – paste your code, see tokens highlighted and errors instantly.
- ✅ **C++‑style** implementation with full support for C++ keywords and literals.
- ✅ **Visual feedback** – animations, status indicators, and a clean terminal‑inspired UI.
